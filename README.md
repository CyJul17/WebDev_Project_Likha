# Likha

**Where Creativity Meets Opportunity**

Likha is a community-based platform designed specifically for artists and clients. It allows artists to showcase their work, connect with potential clients, and manage commissions in a structured and secure environment.

# 1. Introduction

Likha is a website built to support artists by providing them with a dedicated platform where they can present their artwork, gain visibility, and receive commission requests from clients.

Our goal is to create a focused environment where creativity and opportunity meet.

---

# 2. Purpose

## The Problem

Many artists struggle with several issues such as:

* Difficulty gaining visibility
* Trouble finding commission clients
* Lack of protection for their artwork

At the same time, clients and consumers often face problems like:

* Not knowing where to find reliable artists
* Having to search through social media platforms where art is mixed with unrelated content such as drama, memes, and news.

> "There is a need for a dedicated platform made just for artists and clients."
> — BIWOMM 2026

## The Solution

Our solution is **Likha**, a community-based platform where:

* Artists can upload and showcase their artworks
* Users can like, comment, and follow artists
* Clients can request art commissions directly from artists

## Target Users

### Artists

* Students
* Freelance illustrators
* Hobbyists
* Digital creators

### Clients

* Individuals who want custom artwork
* Small businesses
* Content creators

# 6. What Makes Our Website Different?

## Artist-Centered Algorithm (Art-Only Feed)

Unlike typical social media platforms, our feed focuses only on art.

There are:

* No memes
* No unrelated content
* No influencer distractions

The feed prioritizes:

* New artists
* Trending artworks
* Art categories (Digital, Traditional, 3D, etc.)
* User interests

---

## Structured Commission Workflow

On platforms like Instagram or Twitter, commissions usually happen through direct messages which can be messy and hard to track.

Our platform includes:

* Built-in commission request forms
* Order status tracking
  (Pending → In Progress → Completed)
* Automatic receipt generation

---

## Built-in Portfolio System

Each artist profile acts as a complete portfolio including:

* Curated artwork gallery
* Commission pricing display
* Ratings and reviews
* Completed works history

Artists no longer need multiple platforms to showcase their work.

---

## Review and Rating System

After each commission:

* Clients can leave ratings for artists
* Artists can optionally rate clients

Benefits include:

* Encouraging professionalism
* Reducing scam risks
* Building trust between users
